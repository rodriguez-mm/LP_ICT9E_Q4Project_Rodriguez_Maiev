CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Ibarra is a young man of high status who is full of ambition and is the main character of the novel.
  • Ibarra dreams of building a school to help the youth of his town.
Maria Clara
  • Maria Clara is a woman of high status and is Ibarra's love interest.
  • She is described as a perfect Filipina who keeps quiet, prays, and knows how to act like a lady.
Father Damaso
  • Father Damaso is the main antagonist of the novel and is known to be abusive and violent with the public, especially the Ibarra's
  • He is the representation of the ruthlessly corrupt and abusive Spaniards when they invaded our country.
